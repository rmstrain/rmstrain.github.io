Recommendation letter page

Please read this if you'd like me to write you a letter of recommendation. I would need this information at least two weeks before your recommendation letter is due. I also appreciate email reminders that your recommendation letter is due soon.

If I have agreed to write a recommendation letter for you,
then please provide me with all of the below that apply:

  • A copy of your CV/Resume.
  • A copy of your transcript (unofficial or official).
  • Everything that you are submitting with your application. If you are applying for academic jobs send me your research statement, your teaching statement, links to your papers, etc... (drafts, preprints, or published work).
  • If you took courses with me then please remind me of the course(s) and your grade.
  • Provide all required forms (with as much information filled out as possible).
  • Provide any and all letter of recommendation waver forms (usually applies when using envelopes as in the next point). In general you should wave your right to see the recommendation letter because otherwise the place where you are applying will not take the letter as seriously.
  • Stamped and addressed envelopes (if you are asking me to send a mail recommendation).
  • Please tell me who else is writing you a recommendation letter. Please send me a list of names and their job title. (This allows me to write a complementary letter, instead of an overlapping letter, in the situation that they are writing about specific aspects of your application or your work.)
  • If you are applying to a math graduate program, then please send me your GRE scores (both general and subject test scores). If you don't have your scores yet then please send them to me later when you have them.

Also please email me answers to as many of the following questions as you can as soon as possible:

  • What is your year and Major? (For undergrads.)
  • For what are you applying? Please send me a list of the programs and due dates to which you are applying.
  • How would you describe yourself? (This question is very important. A detailed response is very helpful.)
  • What are some of your academic accomplishments?
  • What are some of your nonacademic accomplishments?
  • What are your long terms goals and how would a successful application help you achieve them?
  • Any particular papers/theorems/results or other accomplishments that you want me to emphasize?
  • Anything else that you think I should know for the purposes of writing your recommendation letter?
  • Please be as detailed as possible in in all of your answers. Asking me to peruse your resume/transcript is not so helpful to me in writing your recommendation letter... It is much more helpful for you to write detailed descriptions of things that you want me to know about yourself for this letter.

Please feel free to ask for a meeting to chat with me about these questions, your application, and your recommendation letter. Last but not least, please be sure to send me a reminder at least one week before your recommendation letter is due. If I don't send your letter in time, then I hope that you will remind me another time. I can get very busy.
